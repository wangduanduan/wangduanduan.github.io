enable-100rel#

This enable support for 100rel (100% reliability - PRACK message as defined inRFC3262) This fixes a problem with SIP where provisional messages like “180 Ringing” are not ACK’d and therefore could be dropped over a poor connection without retransmission. 2009-07-08: Enabling this may cause FreeSWITCH to crash, seeFSCORE-392.
